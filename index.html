History of Hanna's Pizza

1980

Founded in February 1980 by Hanna Smyth at home. Hanna was just baking pizza to serve customers around Yonge and Eglinton street.

1990

Hanna's Pizza have decided to have its first store in Dundad Street West over an increase demand of customers.

1996

Hanna's Pizza expand by having a second store in Front Street West.

2001

Hanna's Pizza offers more pizza menu including Hot Banana Peppers and Vegetarian.

2009

Hanna's Pizza launch its 3rd restaurant in Queen Street West to keep up with the growing orders.

2022

Hanna's Pizza 4th restaurant is in construction and expected to launch in late fall 2022. Hanna's Pizza add more piza selection including Meat Lovers and Anchovies.

How We Make Pizza

Serve the pizza.
